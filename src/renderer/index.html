hexClipboard
v1.0.0

A clipboard history manager that sits in your system tray and captures every copy — text, links, and code. Search, pin favourites, and browse your whole history in the signature hexagonal grid.

Shortcuts
Ctrl+Shift+V
Show / hide the window
Ctrl+Shift+H
Open the hex grid view
Ctrl+Shift+X
Toggle private mode (pauses capture)
Ctrl+Shift+P
Pin the last copied item
