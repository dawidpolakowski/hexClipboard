Hexbench
v1.0.0

A honeycomb workbench for your clipboard and notes. Hexbench sits in the tray and captures every copy — text, links, and code — then lets you search, pin, browse the hex grid, gather snippets in the workbench, and keep plain-text or list notes alongside.

Shortcuts
Ctrl+Shift+V
Show / hide the window
Ctrl+Shift+H
Open the hex grid view
Ctrl+Shift+X
Toggle private mode (pauses capture)
Ctrl+Shift+P
Pin the last copied item
